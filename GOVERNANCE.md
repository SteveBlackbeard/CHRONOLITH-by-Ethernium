# Continuity: Project Governance & Resilience 🏛️🧬

This document defines the technical management and long-term sustainability of the **Continuity** framework.

## 1. Decision Protocol
All architectural changes are evaluated via the core **Decision Engine** to ensure alignment with the "Universal Core" principles of simplicity and reliability.

## 2. Succession & Continuity
To mitigate the risk of maintainer abandonment, the project's own continuity files (DECISIONS_LOG.md, STATE.json) serve as a canonical source for future maintainers to resume development with 100% contextual fidelity.

## 3. Contributions
We welcome technical contributions that pass the --strict validation criteria.
